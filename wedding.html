OVERVIEW

Together Wedding Cake is a fictional commercial wedding cake backery with a desire to provide online wedding cake shopping experience. Therefore, the website provides a simulated online shopping experience and allow users to browse through the recent events, store informaiton, how to contact the business, product list, the process of purchasing a product and providing feedback.

SKILLS

Wireframes, mockups, prototyping

TOOLS

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, HTML/CSS/JQuery/Bootstrap

PROCESS

DECISION

Phoebe : chocolate store
Tina : bakery store


We had very similar ideas therefore we first decided to merge both of our ideas and work with a general confectionary store. However, as we were looking for images and color palettes we felt that a confectionery store is too broad and we would rather focus on a single item. At the very end we selected to work with a wedding cake bakery store.

SITE MAP

We came up with a site map which allowed to have better understaning of what pages were essential and how each level of webpages were layouted. We also categorized each page into groups according to grids and types.
